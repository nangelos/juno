About Nick Angelos

Nick Angelos is a second year MBA student at Georgetown University's McDonough School of Business. He spent his summer as an Investment Banking Summer Associate at Bank of America in New York City, and plans to return full-time after graduation.

Before business school Nick was the co-founder of Eddie, a startup in the Home Improvement/Home Repair space, where he focused on software development and financial analysis. Prior to the startup Nick worked in a variety of roles in product development and corporate strategy at Sears Holdings as part of their corporate turnaround.

Nick is a technology and startup enthusiast, and likes to write code whenever an interesting project comes to mind. While his code is primarily (hacky) JavaScript, Nick enjoys getting exposure to other languages and dreams of one day being on par with real software engineers.

Education: MBA from Georgetown University (Expected 2021); BS in Economics from Brigham Young University; FullStack Academy of Code; AS from Dodge City Community College.
